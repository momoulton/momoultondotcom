…historian, writer, digital developer…

Educated at MIT (SB) and Brown University (PhD), I am trained as a historian of modern Britain, Ireland, and the British Empire. I've published widely on these and related topics.

I teach in Harvard University's History and Literature program on topics ranging from inequality, revolution, and reform in 19th century Britain and France to Irish, Scottish, and Welsh politics. I've also taught at Brown and Clark University, and I've worked in academic administration.

I believe we're in the midst of a new print revolution that is changing the way we read, communicate, and think. I'm excited to be a part of it.

I'm available for freelance writing and web development gigs. Contact me if you want to talk.
